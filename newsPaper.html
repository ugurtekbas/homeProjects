School start in Hamburg: mask requirement during breaks

At the start of the school after the summer vacation on Thursday, ... Hamburg's schoolchildren and teachers in the buildings of secondary schools should now wear mouth-nose protection - at least outside of class. Hamburg's school senator Ties Rabe (SPD) said this on Monday morning in an interview with NDR 90.3.

Mask only when sitting down

Only when the students sit down in the classrooms at their place of work or ... when teachers sit at their place of work does the mask requirement cease to apply. "But on the paths, in the school corridors, in the breaks, the masks are to be worn without exception," said Rabe. However, this does not apply to elementary school students up to ten years of age. The risk of infection is significantly lower for them. In addition, they could not yet handle the masks properly.

Talks on the weekend

"We coordinated with leading associations, virologists and experts ... over the weekend, who have now come to the conclusion that a mask requirement makes sense," said Rabe. "That's why Hamburg will introduce such a mask requirement - for everyone who is at school."
